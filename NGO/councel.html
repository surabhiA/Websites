COUNSELLING

Asha Kiran particularly caters to the financially incompetent patients who come for their treatment at S.M.S.Hospital Jaipur (INDIA),a government hospital. In wake of the problems faced by them at the hospital, Asha Kiran initiated counselling and minimum financial help to these patients.

Asha kiran provides monthly ration packages,school/college fees for their children,nursing care, medicines and counselling to patients and family members to boost their morale.We provide these facilities free of cost.In addition, we also counsel the family of patient, about their behaviour towards the patient so that his last days pass peacefully.

Asha Kiran organization helps poor cancer patients by providing them with accommodation, guiding them on how to proceed with treatment, and about precautions and diet. Asha Kiran also empowers them with information regarding government schemes for cancer patients such as, Prime minister’s relief fund and Chief minister’s relief fund, tax benefits, travel concessions etc.

100 % of your donations will be used to help the needy.

How do your donations help Asha Kiran?

Asha Kiran initiated counselling and financial help to needy patients at the outset.It then went on to provide other services like:

  • Awareness
  • Free health check camps
  • Palliative care
  • Rehabilitation

Your donations will majorly help Asha Kiran provide financial help to patients who do not even have the resources to meet their basic needs,let alone treatment for cancer.We provide free medicines to these patients. In addition to pain killers, injections, bandages etc. we also provide for the patient's family's basic need such as food, clothing and shelter.Asha Kiran provides free ration to these families.

Asha Kiran not only looks into the patient's need,but also caters to the patient's family's needs.We help the family rehabilitate themselves incase the patient is the earning member of the family.We help the illiterate women obtain sewing diplomas(or other skills)and provide them with sewing machines so that they can provide for the family.Asha Kiran also sponsors the family's children and helps them complete their basic education.For more information please visit the Sponsor a child page.

How can you donate to Asha Kiran?

Donations are welcome in the form of Cash, Cheque or Demand Draft in the name of ASHA KIRAN. Donations can also be made by directly depositing funds to our bank account.Please Contact us to help Asha Kiran.

100 % of your donations will be used to help the needy.
